# Clinic Website Audit - Sales Guide
## How to Use the Audit Report in Sales Conversations

---

## What You're Selling

You're not selling the audit tool. You're selling **solutions to specific problems that are costing them patients.**

Your edge: You have clinical background + understand what patients need + can spot real problems that generic SEO consultants miss.

---

## The Pitch Framework

### 1. Open with the Problem (Not the Score)
❌ **DON'T:** "Your website scored 41/70"  
✅ **DO:** "Your website makes it hard for patients to book appointments. Most people call you instead of booking online — that friction costs you patients."

### 2. Show Specific Evidence
Pull from the audit report to prove you did your homework:
- "I found 7 services listed but no descriptions — patients don't know what each one is"
- "You have a contact form but no 'Book Online' button — makes it seem like you can't take online appointments"
- "No Google Business Profile link visible — patients searching on Google Maps won't find you"

### 3. Quantify the Impact
- "Clinics with online booking convert 2-3x more patients"
- "Missing Google Business Profile = losing 30-40% of local search traffic"
- "Poor services descriptions = patients leave for competitors with clearer info"

### 4. Give Them the Fix
- "Add Janeapp or Cliniko — real-time booking, cuts friction, patients book immediately"
- "Create a services page with descriptions — patients understand what you offer"
- "Link your Google Business Profile — shows up in Maps, collects reviews, builds credibility"

### 5. Show the Opportunity
"If we fix these three things, you'll get more patient calls, more online bookings, and more reviews. That's how you grow."

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't overwhelm them with jargon**
- Not "Your schema markup is missing LocalBusiness JSON-LD"
- Instead: "Google doesn't know you're a clinic in Ottawa. Let's fix that."

❌ **Don't criticize their design**
- Not "Your site is outdated and ugly"
- Instead: "The layout works, but patients can't book online — that's the issue."

❌ **Don't focus on scores**
- Not "You scored 41/70"
- Instead: "Here are three things costing you patients..."

❌ **Don't promise overnight results**
- Booking improvements: 2-4 weeks
- SEO improvements: 6-12 weeks
- Trust building: Ongoing

✅ **DO focus on patient problems**
✅ **DO give specific evidence**
✅ **DO offer actionable fixes**
✅ **DO explain the impact**
